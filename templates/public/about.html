About HandyBridge

HandyBridge.com is an online marketplace that bridges the gap between home service providers and clients in the Rio Grande Valley and beyond.

Our platform connects skilled professionals — plumbers, cleaners, electricians, and more — with homeowners and renters who need reliable, trustworthy help.

Our Mission

To make finding and booking home services as easy as ordering a ride or booking a stay. We ensure safety through background checks, transparency through reviews, and convenience through automated scheduling and payments.
